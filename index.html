Why We Don't Run Branded Paid

InterContinental Sydney already dominates branded SERPs organically. With limited budget, every dollar is better spent on non-branded terms where we compete for visibility. Branded CPC is low ($1.55–$2.26) but the opportunity cost of diverting budget is too high.

The International Branded Opportunity

Global branded search is +35% above AU on average, and +55% in March. International travellers searching "intercontinental sydney" are high-intent bookers. A targeted test in peak months could capture incremental revenue without cannibalising organic.

  • Mar: 103,490 global vs 66,540 AU (+55%)
  • Oct: 51,270 global vs 36,550 AU (+40%)
  • Sep: 42,030 global vs 29,580 AU (+42%)

July School Holiday Push

Window: June → July
